Log an issue

Logging an issue is a way of alerting your line manager, or another appropriate contact, to something that you've seen or experienced. There are two ways to log an issue.

First, you can log an issue directly. Your details will be sent straight to your chosen contact, who will follow up with you to discuss the problem. They may decide to conduct an informal investigation into the issue.

Second, you can enter the details of your concern into our matching system. We'll hold them securely, unless somebody else comes forward to identify a concern with the same individual. If that happens, we'll send both of your reports on to your points of contact, so that they can be discussed or investigated simultaneously.

In order to log an issue, we'll need a few details from you.

  • The name and email address of the individual responsible for the behaviour
  • The name and email address of an appropriate contact, if not your line manager
  • Your name and email address
