How can we help?

The Ministry of Justice has a zero tolerance policy towards instances of bullying, discrimination, & harassment. Managers will treat allegations seriously. They will investigate all reports and take proportionate action following an investigation. Employees are encouraged to report anything they have seen or experienced.

This online service exists to make that reporting process easier. It is completely confidential: we won't store any identifying information, and nobody will know that you've accessed our service unless you choose to file a named grievance.
