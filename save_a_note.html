Add a private note

The following pages are for you to record the details of anything you've seen or experienced. You may want to make a note of something you've seen, or upload documents - for example, a photo or video - to come back to later.

Once you're finished, we'll save your note, so you can come back at any time if you decide to discuss the issue with your employer. You can also choose to submit your note automatically if someone else reports the same issue. This means that you never have to speak out alone, and you can choose to notify your employer only if somebody else also reports the same individual.
