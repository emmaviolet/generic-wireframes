Would you like us to send this directly?

If you choose to log an issue directly, your details will be sent straight to your chosen contact, who will follow up with you to discuss the problem.

Alternatively, we can hold these details securely, and send them to your contact only if somebody else identifies the same individual. If that happens, we'll send both of your reports on to your points of contact, so that they can be discussed simultaneously.
