Add note to match database

Adding your note to our matching database will really help anyone else who wants to come forward.

In order to enter your note into our database, we'll need a few details from you.

  • Your name and email address
  • Details of the individual responsible for the behaviour, if relevant
  • A decision from you about how you'd like us to send the information
  • Details of an appropriate contact to send your concern to, if not your line manager

One we have those details, we'll hold your note securely, unless somebody else comes forward to identify a concern with the same individual. If that happens, we'll send both of your reports on to your points of contact, so that they can be discussed or investigated together.
