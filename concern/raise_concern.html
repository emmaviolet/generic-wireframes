Raise a concern

Raising a concern is a way of alerting your line manager, or another appropriate contact, to something that you've seen or experienced. This can be anything at all that worries you, but may include, for example, details of someone you believe is bullying, or being bullied.

Over the next few pages, we'll ask for a few details from you:

  • Your name and email address
  • Details of what you've seen or experienced
  • Details of the individual responsible for the behaviour, if relevant
  • A decision from you about how you'd like us to send the information
  • Details of an appropriate contact to send your concern to, if not your line manager

You'll be able to review all this information before we send it.
